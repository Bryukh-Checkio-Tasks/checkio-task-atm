Stephen stopped weeding the garden and looked up at Sofia.

“What kind of ship?”

“I see...” Stephen grinned as he reluctantly continued weeding. “You know, it’s thanks to you that my parents are making me pull every weed from garden as punishment; my dad is picking the truck up from the garage as we speak. Sofia let Stephen continue rambling and grumbling because his gripes were extremely entertaining to her. Also, she knew that, deep down in the bottom of his heart, he loved the adventuring and was always up for more. A smile crept across her face as she helped him to finish the chores.
